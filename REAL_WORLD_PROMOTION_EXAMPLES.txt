## üéØ **How Targeting Works for Each Type:**

### **BOGO Promotions:**
- **Buy Items**: `buy_target_type`, `buy_target_category_id`, `buy_target_product_ids`
- **Get Items**: `get_target_type`, `get_target_category_id`, `get_target_product_ids`

### **Happy Hour, Percentage Off, Fixed Off:**
- **Items**: `target_type`, `target_category_id`, `target_product_ids`
- **Time**: `start_time`, `end_time`, `days_of_week` (for Happy Hour)

## üîß **The System Flow:**

1. **User creates promotion** with specific targeting
2. **System stores** the targeting rules
3. **When calculating orders**, system checks which items qualify
4. **Promotion applies** only to qualifying items
